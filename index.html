Milanese con pure

Description Of Recipe

This food is realized with chicken and Shredded bread

This recipe is traditionally made in South America and is easy to create

List of ingredients

  • chicken
  • papa
  • egg
  • carrot
  • Shredded bread
  • garlic
  • parsley
  • milk

    • Step by Step

    1. Use a container
    2. Mix eggs with garlic and parsley
    3. Introduce the chicken en the mix
    4. Leave reposed for 10 min
    5. dip the chicken in the breadcrumbs
    6. Bake for 20 minutes at 180 degrees
